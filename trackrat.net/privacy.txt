Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 25, 2026

TrackRat is a transit tracking app for NJ Transit, Amtrak, PATH, and PATCO trains.

1. Information We Collect

Local Data (stored on your device only):
- Favorite stations
- Recent trip history
- App preferences

Server Data (sent to our servers):
- Push notification tokens for Live Activity updates
- Anonymous usage data for service improvement

We do not collect names, email addresses, or other personally identifiable information through the app.

2. Subscriptions and Payments

All subscription purchases are processed by Apple through the App Store. We do not receive or store your payment information. Apple's privacy policy governs payment data: https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/

3. How We Use Information

- Push tokens: To send real-time train updates to your Live Activities
- Anonymous usage: To improve service reliability and performance

4. Data Storage and Retention

- Local data remains on your device until you delete the app
- Push tokens are retained only while you have active Live Activities
- Anonymous usage data is retained for up to 90 days

5. Third-Party Services

- Apple: Payment processing and push notifications
- We do not sell or share your data with advertisers

6. Your Rights

You can:
- Delete local data by removing the app
- Disable push notifications in iOS Settings
- Cancel subscriptions via Settings > Apple ID > Subscriptions

7. Cookies and Tracking

The app does not use cookies or cross-app tracking technologies.

8. Security

We use industry-standard encryption (HTTPS) for all server communications.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted here.

10. Contact Us

Questions about this privacy policy: trackrat@andymartin.cc
